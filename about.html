About Me

I am a researcher in the lab of Fan Wang at Duke University. I study sensory and motor circuits involved in touch and pain.

I completed my BSE and MS in Biomedical Engineering with a focus in Electrical Engineering at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. I am finishing my MD/PhD at Duke University in Durham, North Carolina.

My technical research interests include electrophysiology, microscopy, and experimental hardware development. I strongly support efforts for reproducibility and technique dissemination in science.
