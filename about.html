About Me

I am a researcher in the lab of Fan Wang at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I study sensory and motor circuits involved in touch and pain.

I completed my BSE and MS in Biomedical Engineering with a focus in Electrical Engineering at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. I completed my MD/PhD at Duke University in Durham, North Carolina in 2021.

My technical research interests include electrophysiology, microscopy, and experimental hardware development. I strongly support efforts for reproducibility and technique dissemination in science.
